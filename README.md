# rosalind-bioinformatics
Welcome to my **Rosalind Info** repository! This project is a curated collection of programming solutions for bioinformatics challenges hosted on the [Rosalind](https://rosalind.info/) platform. The primary goal of this repo is to demonstrate how algorithmic problem-solving can unravel complex biological data.

## 🧬 Computational Biology & Research Focus

The solutions housed here are heavily geared toward computational biology problems involving nucleic acid (DNA, RNA) and protein analysis. (See HAMM.py, PROT.py, ORF.py, for example).

This focus stems from my deep interest in the human genome and my practical research background. Over the summer, I conducted pyrimidine research at the **UAlbany RNA Institute**, where I worked firsthand with molecular structures and sequencing concepts. This repo bridges that wet-lab and structural biology perspective with efficient, programmatic solutions to fundamental genomic problems.
