# rosalind-bioinformatics
Welcome to my **Rosalind Info** repository! This project is a collection of programming solutions for bioinformatics challenges hosted on the [Rosalind](https://rosalind.info/) platform. The primary goal of this repo is to develop my own python fluency through problems directly relevant to bioinformatics/computational biology.

## Computational Biology & Research Focus
The solutions housed here are geared toward computational biology problems involving nucleic acid (DNA, RNA) and protein analysis. (See HAMM.py, PROT.py, ORF.py, for example).

This focus stems from my interest in the human genome and my practical background in nucleic acid research. Over the summer, I conducted pyrimidine research at the **UAlbany RNA Institute**, where I worked firsthand with molecular structures and sequencing concepts. This repo bridges that wet-lab and biochemical perspective with efficient, programmatic solutions to fundamental genomic problems.
